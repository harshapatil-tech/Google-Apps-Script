Given the increasing instances of data breaches and cybersecurity issues, our customers have significantly increased their investment in and vigilance toward data security and unauthorized access. Therefore, based on our customers' recommendations, UpThink has implemented additional protocols to monitor the quality of work and prevent unauthorized access to data. These protocols are executed through the following policies:

1. Employee productivity software (Teramind)
2. BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Policy

Teramind is a user activity, compliance, and insider threat prevention software. We are required to share Teramind reports with our customers to ensure that we are delivering our work in adherence to global standards. Teramind helps us record screen activity and track time for quality control for certain client centric roles.

The BYOD policy outlines the do's and don'ts of using personal or official devices both within and outside the office.
